About Me

Hi, This is Oshin

I am not a professional rather a self made artist,and my art is manifestation of my journey in this life like road.Every piece I work on has a deeper connection with my inner self which also reflects a vision to revolutionize world.In every picture I put an effort to replicate love,care,compassion,for every living creature on earth and its meant to motivate and go through a journey of adventure and exploration the moment you go through the colors of my art.It will challlenge your preconceptions and will make you look at the bold and beautiful side of my art.It's not a job or work to make a piece of art,I try to portray those social and emotional situations of our life that cannot expressed in words and can be felt from colors and emotions of my paintings.My art speaks not only for happy minds but also for those who were silenced long ago,and each work of mine will take you to a tour of good and bad times in your life only when you try to dive deeper into it.What you will perceive and learn out of my art will make me artist in true sense.If you also have interest in sketching and painting just do it,don't think how will it look or how to draw,just hold the brush and start making it see the magic your story will make on a blank piece of paper which will be later worth million dollars to you and to the consince of billions of people out there.Every composition will walk you through various stages of our life since i have been creating those amazing things since childhood.

Every imperfections is a path taken towards perfection
